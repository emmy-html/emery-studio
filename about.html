Meet the Creator

Emery Jordan [she/they] is a writer and multi-disciplinary artist from the chicagoland area. she makes collages, poetry, illustrations, and sequential art about lesbian/dyke culture, afro-futurism and mental health. their work explores themes of Black culture, queer/lesbian identity, mental health, and spirituality. after viewing her work, think about the intersections of technology, identity, and culture in your own life.
